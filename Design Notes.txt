DATABASE
   MongoDB(NoSQL)
      Will utilize 2 schemas:
      book(Title,Author,ISBN,Department, Class)
      Post(email, type, book, date)
   - Schemas will be implemented using Mongoose(http://mongoosejs.com/docs/guide.html)
